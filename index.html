Play Game
KidsPlay is a social networking application for iOS and Android designed to facilitate social interaction among children in communities, as well as provide a tool for parents to keep tabs on their children. Too many children these days grow up with the inability to communicate well with others, a skill that is a large asset when it comes to succeeding as an adult. With our application, parents can create events for local families to attend or view and bring their kids to nearby events already created. Parents may also send messages to other parents through our app, for the purpose of creating a network between the parents, so that no parent falls out of the loop when it comes to their kids and their neighborhood. Parents may also keep close eye on their kids at all times using our child tracker, a feature only the parent of the child can use, that tracks where their kid is and has been. Our main goal is to bring communities together, give parents more peace of mind, and better prepare the next generation for the future.
